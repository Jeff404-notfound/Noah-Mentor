Stress, Anxiety & Depression Questionnaires

Here are four scientifically validated questionnaires for stress, anxiety, and depression, with sample questions and response options.

1. Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9) for Depression

9-item self-report measure to screen for depression over the last two weeks.

Sample Questions:
  • Little interest or pleasure in doing things?
  • Feeling down, depressed, or hopeless?
  • Trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much?
  • Feeling tired or having little energy?
  • Poor appetite or overeating?
Response Options:

Not at all | Several days | More than half the days | Nearly every day

2. Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7 (GAD-7) for Anxiety

7-item questionnaire measuring severity of generalized anxiety disorder over the past two weeks.

Sample Questions:
  • Feeling nervous, anxious, or on edge?
  • Not being able to stop or control worrying?
  • Worrying too much about different things?
  • Trouble relaxing?
  • Becoming easily annoyed or irritable?
Response Options:

Not at all | Several days | More than half the days | Nearly every day

3. Perceived Stress Scale (PSS-10) for Stress

10-item instrument to measure perception of stress over the past month.

Sample Questions:
  • How often have you been upset because of something unexpected?
  • How often have you felt unable to control important things in life?
  • How often have you felt nervous and "stressed"?
  • How often have you felt confident about handling personal problems?
  • How often have you felt difficulties piling up beyond control?
Response Options:

Never | Almost Never | Sometimes | Fairly Often | Very Often

4. Depression, Anxiety and Stress Scale (DASS-21)

21-item self-report assessing three emotional states: depression, anxiety, stress.

Sample Questions:
  • I found it hard to wind down. (Stress)
  • I couldn't seem to experience any positive feeling at all. (Depression)
  • I was worried about situations in which I might panic. (Anxiety)
  • I felt that I was rather touchy. (Stress)
  • I felt down-hearted and blue. (Depression)
Response Options:

0: Did not apply at all | 1: Somewhat | 2: Considerably | 3: Very much/most of the time
