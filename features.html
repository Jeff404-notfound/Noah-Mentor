🌟 Noah AI Features

Discover What Noah AI Offers

Our platform is built to support youth mental wellness with the right blend of AI and human care. Explore the full set of features below:

🧠 AI-Powered Interaction

  • Natural language conversations (empathetic and supportive responses).
  • Context-aware memory for personalized chats.
  • Multilingual support (English, Hindi, etc.) to reach diverse users.
  • Voice input/output (for accessibility).

💬 Mental Health & Wellness

  • Mood tracking (daily check-ins).
  • Stress/anxiety/depression symptom reflection (not diagnosis, but awareness).
  • Guided exercises (breathing, mindfulness, journaling prompts).
  • Motivational quotes and wellness tips.

📅 Task & Routine Support

  • Habit tracking (sleep, study, exercise).
  • Smart reminders for self-care (hydration, breaks, journaling).
  • Daily planner with productivity suggestions.

🛡️ Safety & Ethics

  • Crisis detection & redirection to helplines (e.g., when user mentions self-harm).
  • Anonymous & private chats (no data misuse).
  • Content moderation (prevent harmful outputs).

🎨 User Experience

  • Friendly chatbot persona with adaptive tone (empathetic, not robotic).
  • Gamification (streaks, badges for healthy habits).
  • Personal dashboard (progress visualization, mood history).

🔗 Integration & Accessibility

  • Login with Google, GitHub, or Email (like your Timelyze project).
  • Cloud storage for user activity history.
  • Mobile-first design (accessible on phone browsers).
  • Offline mode (basic journaling without internet).
